About Us

One of the biggest achievements in life is considered to be building one’s own home. Whether you are building a home on a plot or have recently purchased an apartment flat, makemyhome.com helps you with all your planning and design needs without needing a time consuming and expensive visit to an engineer or a designer.

MMH has a large collection of house plans and elevations to chose from. The collection is entirely free to browse for your next home building idea. Once you like an idea it is easy to convert it into a complete set of drawings by adding that plan to your cart at MMH. These drawings can be used to hire a contractor and start the construction process without having to hire an architect or a civil engineer. You may also engage MMH to create a totally customized house plan at a fraction of cost. You may also use MMH drawings in parallel to the drawings that your architect provides. Having a couple of options handy is a great idea before you make that dream house. After all, building a house is an event that takes place only 1 or 2 times in one’s lifetime. Plan wisely and plan with a lot of options.
